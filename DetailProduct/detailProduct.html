Bagel is a bread originating in the Jewish communities of Poland. It is shaped by hand into the form of a ring from yeasted wheat dough, roughly hand-sized. It is dense, chewy, doughy interior with a browned and crisp exterior. Bagels are topped with seeds baked on the outer crust.
